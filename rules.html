Instructions

  • Start the game by pressing any key on your keyboard.
  • Watch the flashing button – it’s the game’s move.
  • Click the buttons in the same sequence.
  • With each level, the sequence gets longer.
  • Game ends if you make a mistake!
  • Try to beat your highest score.
